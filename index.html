Learning to Dance

Hi, I'm Lenny. I learned to Lindy Lop quite reluctantly. My wife wanted to learn to dance. I did my best to enjoy what a good time she was having until one day I happened to notice I, too, was having a good time. I've been an enthusiastic lindy hopper ever since.

A little about the history of lindy...

Various types of swing dance developed all over the country in the early half of the 20th century. Where Lindy hop first appeared is the subject of some debate but it evolved in Harlem in the fertile ground of the Harlem Renaissance and the heyday of the jazz scene in New York. Eventually landing in Hollywood, it would be seen an embraced by kids across the country.

Oh, the music...

Jazz has been called the first american artform. Getting to know early jazz changed my appreciation for american music, music more generally, and changed my dancing. This well runs deep.
